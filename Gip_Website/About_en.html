My name is Kyano De Maertelaere and I am 17 years old. I come from a family with two brothers and a sister. I am currently studying computer science at Atheneum Eeklo, a school in Eeklo, Belgium.

My hobby is gaming. It is something I put a lot of time into and I always enjoy it. It gives me the chance to escape daily reality for a while and concentrate on something I love. What is even more fun is to meet up with friends to game. Laughing together, coming up with strategies and challenging each other makes it even more fun than when I play alone.

I enjoy learning how to create software to solve problems. Later, I would like to to work in the technology industry and continue to grow as a programmer.

I've never really gone on holiday to other countries because it's not easy with a family of six. Travelling with a large family can be a difficulty because It can be hard to plan a holiday that is fun for everyone. And because it can be difficult to arrange everything like plane tickets and rental cars.

I am looking forward to exploring new places and cultures during my internship. It will give me practical experience and professional skills and give me a chance to leave my comfort zone. I am curious about the future and think this internship will be a valuable step in my career.
